## Usage Notes

1. **User Tier Consistency**: Always use "High Spender / Mid Spender / Low Spender" consistently. Avoid mixing with "whale/dolphin/minnow" in formal output.

2. **Currency Format**: Use USD with standard psychological pricing ($0.99, $4.99, $9.99, etc.)

3. **Percentage Format**: Use "%" symbol after the number (5%, not 5 percent)

4. **Date Format**: Use ISO format (yyyy-MM-dd) or US format (MM/dd/yyyy) consistently within each skill

5. **Metric Names**: Keep acronyms in English (ARPU, ARPPU, LTV, DAU, MAU)
